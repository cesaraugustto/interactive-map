Interactive map for Middle Earth, the greatest work of fiction and fantasy ever written.

This is a work made by fans for fans. The administrative team of the literary community Tolkieniano Brasil is developing a web application that will help several readers to locate themselves during moments of doubt.

Future points to be added: a) Distance between locations. It is possible to trace in real-world comparisons how far the characters have walked. Ex: Silmarillion, page 140, edition Martins Fontes Br. Tolkien states that the distance between Menegroth and Angband is approximately 150 leagues.
